Research Identity

Ritam Pandey is an Electronics and Communication Engineering student operating at the frontier of hardware intelligence — designing systems that think, move, communicate, and adapt.

His work spans autonomous robotic platforms built for cross-domain deployment, modular embedded hardware architectures that reconfigure on demand, and decentralized communication frameworks engineered to function without infrastructure.

Each patented system represents a solution to a fundamental engineering challenge — from multi-terrain rovers to zero-trust mesh networks — built with the discipline of hardware design and the precision of protocol engineering.
